Welcome to Penn GSE! 
As we navigate the path ahead, this newsletter will serve as your compass, guiding you through important dates, academic resources, and campus events. Orientation Week! 
With an abundance of new friendships, enriching learning experiences, and an infectious spirit of excitement, we've wrapped up an extraordinary Orientation Week. What a phenomenal beginning to the year!

Important Reminders
  • Complete all your health insurance and immunization requirements and update it on Wellness @ Penn! 
  • Important Dates to remember
     
    • Final day to add/drop a class with NO financial penalty is Tuesday, September 12 (two weeks from today).
    • Last day to finish the Healthy Relationship & Violence Prevention section along with the consent forms on the Penn Experience is Friday, September 15.
    • Last day to complete Modules 1 – 3 of the Penn experience without losing the ability to enroll in spring courses is Sunday, October 15.
    • Last day to apply for December graduation is Tuesday, November 28.

Helpful Resources
 
  • The Graduate Student Center: located at 3615 Locust Walk, provides all Penn graduate and professional students with a central space on campus to study, relax, and meet up with – and make – friends from across the University. Students can fill up on free coffee and tea (don’t forget your reusable mug!)
 
  • Student Health & Counseling is here to support your physical and emotional well-being through high-quality, compassionate care 
 
  •  Financial Wellness @ Penn offers appointments every Monday at the Grad Center to review your budget and talk through financial goals.
 
  •  Weingarten Center: provides academic support tailored to your needs. Make sure to take a look at the Strategies for Success in Graduate Studies series, as well as the Academics Plus workshops for international students. Both series are free and open to all graduate and professional students.
 
  • Graduate Student Reading Room Van Pelt Library Class of 1937 Reading Room, 5th Floor 
    Includes 34 carrels, 2 large reading tables, and soft seating for 8. 
    Daily during Library hours; PennCard required for entrance 

Exploring One Penn GSE


This indispensable site is full of resources for the Penn GSE community, including the Office of Student Success, the Office of Student Records, the Dean’s office, IT, and many organizations across GSE and campus.

  It is also where you will find GSE policies, the GSE academic calendar, and many of the forms you will need through your time at here. 

Most general questions can be answered at this site.  Before classes start ramping up, please take some time to click around, and then bookmark this site!

Group Study Spaces Available!

We are excited that you can reserve the group study spaces on the first floor of the 3700 Walnut Street building. 

Starting this week, GSE students will be able to reserve these rules under the following conditions:

  • Group study room spaces are designed for active collaboration. Students agree to abide by the Penn Codes of Student Conduct and to follow GSE policies. Use is restricted to Penn GSE ID holders.
  • Use of Group Study Rooms requires 2 or more people.
  • Individuals may reserve a room for group use for up to two hours a week.
  • Log into Robin via Single Sign-On (SSO) with your PennKey to reserve a room beginning on September 5.
  • Reservations can be made seven days in advance.
  • Individuals may not reserve study rooms for private study but may use unreserved rooms temporarily until groups with reservations arrive.
  • Submit a ticket to GSE-IT if you have issues with the Robin reservation system.
If you use ANY space inside GSE complex, please make sure to clean up after yourself to ensure that the space can be enjoyed by all!
